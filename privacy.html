Privacy Policy for DealSpot

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

DealSpot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the DealSpot Shop Mini application (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

DealSpot operates within the Shopify Shop app ecosystem. We collect the following information:

  • Product Data: We display product information including prices, discounts, ratings, and reviews provided by Shopify and participating merchants. This data is provided through Shopify's Shop Minis SDK.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the Service, such as products viewed and deals accessed. This helps us improve the Service.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics may be collected to understand how the Service is used.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Service
  • Display relevant deals and product recommendations
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information:

  • With Shopify in accordance with their policies and the Shop Minis SDK
  • With merchants whose products are displayed in the Service (through Shopify's systems)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your data is handled through Shopify's secure infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain usage data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (subject to Service functionality)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with Shopify and operates within the Shopify Shop app. Your use of the Service is also subject to Shopify's privacy policies and terms of service.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
